TechCon 2024 brought together experts, researchers, and industry practitioners to explore the forefront of innovation in surface engineering, coatings, thin films, and vacuum technology. The event ran from May 6 to May 9 in Chicago and featured a rich technical program, tutorials, technology exhibits, discussion forums, and networking opportunities. Attendees experienced keynote presentations, hands-on problem-solving tutorials, and over 150 exhibiting companies showcasing advances in deposition, surface treatments, and related research. The conference also included social events, a 5K fun run, and award ceremonies, creating both professional and collegial opportunities for participants.
